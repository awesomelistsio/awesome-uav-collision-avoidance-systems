> A curated list of frameworks, libraries, research papers, datasets, and tools for Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) collision avoidance systems.

Collision avoidance systems are essential for the safe operation of UAVs (drones) in both controlled and uncontrolled airspace. These systems prevent mid-air collisions and ground impacts through sensors, algorithms, and communication protocols.

## Frameworks & Libraries

- [Paparazzi UAV](https://wiki.paparazziuav.org/wiki/Main_Page) – Open-source UAV system supporting sense-and-avoid modules.
- [OpenUAV](https://github.com/uncc-uav-lab/OpenUAV) – Framework supporting UAV swarm control and collision avoidance.
- [PX4 Avoidance](https://github.com/PX4/PX4-Avoidance) – Real-time onboard obstacle avoidance using stereo vision and LiDAR for PX4 autopilot systems.
- [AirSim](https://github.com/microsoft/AirSim) – Drone simulator with support for collision avoidance algorithm testing.

## Open-Source Projects

- [UAV Collision Avoidance Using ROS](https://github.com/gsilano/UAV_collision_avoidance) – Implementation of collision avoidance systems using ROS and Gazebo.
- [MAVROS Obstacle Avoidance](https://github.com/mavlink/mavros) – Middleware connecting MAVLink-compatible drones with ROS-based obstacle avoidance systems.

## Research Papers

- [Survey of UAV Collision Avoidance Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.04248) – Comprehensive survey covering sensors, algorithms, and regulatory considerations.
- [Real-Time Collision Avoidance for UAVs](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/8793280) – IEEE publication on real-time methods for drone collision avoidance.
- [Sense and Avoid Technologies for UAVs](https://ntrs.nasa.gov/citations/20110008108) – NASA technical report on sense-and-avoid systems for drones.
- [Vision-Based Collision Avoidance](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0967066118300025) – Research on computer vision methods for UAV collision prevention.

## Datasets

- [Drone Detection Dataset](https://github.com/dataset-ninja/drone-dataset) – Useful for training machine learning models on drone collision scenarios.
- [UAV Obstacle Detection Dataset](https://projects.asl.ethz.ch/datasets/doku.php?id=multi_agent_quadrotor) – ETH Zurich multi-UAV dataset with obstacle information.

## Simulation Tools

- [Gazebo](https://gazebosim.org/) – Open-source robotics simulator commonly used for UAV collision avoidance research.
- [RotorS Simulator](https://github.com/ethz-asl/rotors_simulator) – UAV simulation platform for ROS.
- [USSim (UAV Swarm Simulator)](https://github.com/AICPS/USSim) – MATLAB-based simulator supporting multi-UAV collision scenarios.

## Community & Learning

- [PX4 Discuss: Avoidance](https://discuss.px4.io/c/avoidance/14) – PX4 community discussions on UAV obstacle avoidance.
- [ROS Discourse](https://discourse.ros.org/) – General robotics and UAV-related threads including collision avoidance systems.
- [UAV Coach Blog](https://uavcoach.com/blog/) – Educational content on drone technology, including safety systems.
